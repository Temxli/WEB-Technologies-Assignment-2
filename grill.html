Grilled Steak

Indulge in the rich and fudgy goodness of chocolate brownies. These decadent treats are perfect for satisfying your sweet cravings.

Ingredients
  • 2 boneless ribeye steaks
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • Fresh rosemary sprigs for garnish
Instructions
  1. Preheat your grill to high heat.
  2. Brush steaks with olive oil and rub with minced garlic.
  3. Season both sides with salt and freshly ground black pepper.
  4. Grill steaks for 4-5 minutes on each side for medium-rare, or adjust cooking time to your desired doneness.
  5. Remove from grill and let rest for a few minutes.
  6. Garnish with fresh rosemary sprigs before serving.
Grilled Lemon Herb Chicken

Experience the bright and zesty flavors of grilled lemon herb chicken, tender and packed with fresh herbs.

Ingredients
  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • Zest and juice of 2 lemons
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons fresh basil, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
Instructions
  1. In a bowl, combine lemon zest, lemon juice, olive oil, minced garlic, basil, parsley, salt, and black pepper to make a marinade.
  2. Place chicken breasts in a resealable plastic bag and pour the marinade over them. Seal the bag and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  4. Remove chicken from the marinade and grill for about 6-7 minutes per side or until cooked through.
  5. Serve with extra lemon wedges and fresh herbs.
Grilled Vegetable Skewers

Elevate your grilling game with colorful and flavorful vegetable skewers, perfect as a side or a vegetarian main.

Ingredients
  • Assorted vegetables (e.g., bell peppers, zucchini, cherry tomatoes, red onions, mushrooms)
  • Olive oil
  • Balsamic vinegar
  • Garlic powder
  • Dried oregano
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
Instructions
  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Cut vegetables into bite-sized pieces.
  3. Thread the vegetables onto skewers, alternating between types.
  4. In a bowl, whisk together olive oil, balsamic vinegar, garlic powder, dried oregano, salt, and black pepper to make a marinade.
  5. Brush the marinade over the vegetable skewers.
  6. Grill for about 10-12 minutes, turning occasionally, until vegetables are tender and have grill marks.
  7. Serve as a colorful and delicious side dish.
