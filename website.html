Lasagna

Lasagne comes from the Italian city of Naples, though the first references to what we now think of as the dish came in a 14th century English cookbook.

Ingredients Instructions
  • Garlic
  • Ground beef
  • Spaghetti sauce
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cottage cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
