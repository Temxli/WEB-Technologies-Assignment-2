Margherita Pizza

The classic Margherita pizza is a timeless favorite with a simple yet elegant combination of fresh ingredients.

Ingredients

For a delicious Margherita pizza, you'll need:

  • 1 pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • Fresh mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Olive oil
Instructions

Here's how to make Margherita pizza:

  1. Roll out the pizza dough into a circle.
  2. Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce on the dough.
  3. Add slices of fresh mozzarella cheese evenly.
  4. Scatter fresh basil leaves on top.
  5. Drizzle with olive oil.
  6. Bake in a preheated oven at 475°F (245°C) for about 10-12 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
Pepperoni Pizza

Pepperoni pizza is a crowd-pleaser with its savory blend of zesty tomato sauce, gooey cheese, and spicy pepperoni slices.

Ingredients

To make a delicious Pepperoni Pizza, you'll need:

  • 1 pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Sliced pepperoni
Instructions

Here's how to make Pepperoni Pizza:

  1. Roll out the pizza dough into a circle.
  2. Spread a generous layer of tomato sauce on the dough.
  3. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese.
  4. Layer on pepperoni slices.
  5. Bake in a preheated oven at 475°F (245°C) for 10-12 minutes or until the cheese is bubbly and the crust is golden.
BBQ Chicken Pizza

BBQ chicken pizza combines the smoky goodness of barbecue sauce with tender chicken, red onions, and a sprinkle of cilantro.

Ingredients

For a delicious BBQ Chicken Pizza, you'll need:

  • 1 pizza dough
  • Barbecue sauce
  • Cooked and shredded chicken breast
  • Sliced red onions
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish
Instructions

Here's how to make BBQ Chicken Pizza:

  1. Roll out the pizza dough into a circle.
  2. Brush on a layer of barbecue sauce.
  3. Add cooked and shredded chicken breast.
  4. Sprinkle with sliced red onions and shredded mozzarella cheese.
  5. Bake in a preheated oven at 475°F (245°C) for 10-12 minutes or until the cheese is melted and the crust is crisp.
  6. Garnish with fresh cilantro before serving.
